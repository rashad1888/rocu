About

At ROCO Martial Arts Academy, we think that learning martial arts can empower people. Our school provides a thorough education that fosters mental and physical fortitude as well as a profound regard for oneself and other people. ROCO offers a friendly and encouraging atmosphere for everyone, regardless of experience level. Whether you are a novice trying to learn the fundamentals or an accomplished practitioner hoping to hone your abilities. 

 At ROCO, our goal is to create an environment where people may reach their full potential. Using martial arts, we hope to foster resilience, instill confidence, and encourage lifelong fitness. We are dedicated to provide top-notch tuition that incorporates cutting-edge training methods with a traditional foundation.

 

Kids Classes 

 Kids' lessons: We teach the foundations of martial arts in a fun and interesting approach in our kids' lessons. Coordination, self-discipline, and confidence are the main goals of these classes.

Adult Classes 

Adult programmes: We provide self-defense, fitness, and personal development programmes for individuals of all ability levels. Our knowledgeable teachers offer tailored advice to assist you in achieving your objectives.  

Family Classes 

Family Classes: At ROCO, we think that training families together makes them stronger. Our family classes provide a special chance for parents and kids to be together while teaching them useful martial arts skills.

Specialized workshops   

Specialised Workshops: Throughout the year, we provide a range of workshops, such as advanced technique clinics, boot camps for fitness, and lectures on self-defense. These workshops offer the chance for specialised instruction and skill development.

OUR INSTRUCTORS

Specialised seminars: We provide a variety of seminars all year long, including lectures on self-defense, advanced technique clinics, and fitness boot camps. These workshops provide the opportunity for skill development and specific teaching.
